2. Principle: No collection and processing of personal data

The app is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, process, store or transmit personal data.

Purpose of processing

The app is used exclusively for the local processing of PDF documents and other files provided by the user on their device.

Storage

All generated or processed data remains exclusively on the user's local device storage. There is no upload to external servers, clouds, or to us.

Internet connection

The app does not require an active internet connection for its functions. Unintentional or covert data transmission is technically impossible.

3. Required permissions of the app

To perform the functions requested by the user, the app requires access to specific resources of the Android device. These permissions are used exclusively for the purpose stated below and serve to fulfill the terms of use and provide the app functionality (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR).

Required permissions

The app requires access to local device storage to read user files and save results locally.

Purpose

Reading the PDF files selected by the user from the local device storage; Saving the results of PDF processing locally to the device storage.

The permission is not used to monitor the user or to secretly transmit data. All processing takes place exclusively locally on the user's device.

4. Disclosure of data to third parties

As the app does not collect personal data, disclosure to third parties is excluded.

5. Data subject rights

You have rights under the GDPR. Since we do not store personal data, these rights primarily concern contacting the controller.

  • Right to access (Art. 15 GDPR): You have the right to request information about whether and which personal data about you is being processed.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): You have the right to request the immediate rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): You have the right to request the deletion of your data stored by us.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR).
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR).
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR).

Note: Since the app does not collect or store data, these rights are in practice mainly directed towards the possibility of contacting the controller (see point 1).

6. Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR)

Without prejudice to any other administrative or judicial remedy, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

7. Changes to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy when necessary.
