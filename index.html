Privacy Policy – PdfToolBox

Last updated: 18 Dec 2025

1. Controller

PdfToolBox (Serhat Digital) is the controller responsible for the processing activities described in this notice. For privacy inquiries, please provide your official contact email and we will update this section accordingly.

2. Data Processed

We process files (PDFs, images) locally on your device. We do not upload your content to our servers. We do not collect server‑side usage logs or analytics.

3. Permissions

We request file access solely to allow you to select, open, and process PDFs. No other sensitive permissions are used.

4. Storage & Retention

On Android, iOS, and Windows, files remain on the device. We do not maintain any persistent server‑side storage. Temporary files are removed by the operating system or when you close the app.

5. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your data with third parties. The app contains no advertising, tracking pixels, or analytics SDKs.

6. Payments

No web‑based payments are used. If payment features are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated before they become available.

7. Data Security

Processing is performed locally without central servers. We rely on platform security features (sandboxing, HTTPS for external resources where applicable) to protect your data.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not store personal data on our servers, common access or deletion requests largely do not apply. For any privacy‑related request, please contact us via the email address above once provided.

9. Changes

We may update this policy if features change. Material updates will be reflected in this page and, where appropriate, communicated in‑app.
